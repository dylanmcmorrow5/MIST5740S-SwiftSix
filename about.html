The University of Georgia Club Cross Country Team is made up of undergraduate students, graduate students, and employees at the University of Georgia who run and compete together at the club level. he club races against other collegiate club teams in association with NIRCA (National Intercollegiate Running Club Association [http://www.clubrunning.org/]). Throughout the year the team trains for and travels to various meets across the country. The club includes runners of all skill levels from beginner to expert. Anyone can join the club, run workouts, and compete in NIRCA qualifier meets. This club is a great way to connect with other student runners at the University of Georgia and at other universities.
